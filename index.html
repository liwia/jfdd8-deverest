WHAT DO WE OFFER?

WIXER is a professional party planner for every occasion. With our APP you’ll be able to organize everything exactly as you want! And what You don’t want is a ton of stickies lying around your house. So just follow these FEW SIMPLE STEPS and your perfect party plan will be there.

All types of events:
Ability to schedule any event such as birthdays, meetings with friends etc.

Calendar:
Create your own event or join to existing.

You specify the number of people:
It can be several or even several hundred people.

Invitations:
You invite anyone you want!

Shopping list:
Make a list of the things you need and distribute it between interested people.

Gift list:
Choosing gifts can be fun.

Reminders:
Possibility to send reminders before each event.

Ranking:
Ranking of the best events.

Gallery:
Gallery of organized events.
